Papusa

A Pupusa is a delicious thick corn masa Flatbread filled cheese, beans, meat. It hails from El Salvador and is their national Dish. A Pupusa is also accompanied by curtido (cabbage slaw), and tomato salsa(not spicy)

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of bacon
  • 3 tomatoes
  • 1 cup of monterey jack or mozzeralla cheese
  • 1 green pepper
    • <1>2 cloves of garlic (optional) <1i>salt to taste
  • 4 cups of masa harina
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
