Recipe for Oven Backed BBQ Chicken Wings

BBQ Chicken wings are one of the most popular and most heard of dish ever made, everyone loves them even people that don't eat meat wish they could eat them. It's on every bar food menu and sold anywhere you can get chicken. Best thing of all, they are easy to make and you can use any tpye of BBQ sauce you like. Straight up classic forever!!

Ingredients

  • Any type of BBQ sauce you like
  • olive oil
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Chicken Wings
  • Big Bowl for tossing
  • Baking Sheet
  • parchment paper
  • Oven

Steps for cooking and preping these wings

  1. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees and line your baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. If your wings arent already seprated, get a knife and cut the wings off at there joints, throw those bastards into the big bowl.
  3. In a large bowl pour salt, pepper and olive oil into the bowl with the wings and start tossing the wings until they covered.(tossing is easier then it looks)
  4. Line up the wings on the backing sheet in a single layer and Bake until the wings are crispy browned for around 45-60 mins.(Be checking them!!)
  5. After they are nice and crispy the way you like them, take off the sheet and throw back in the bowl(clean) with your favorite BBQ sauce. Toss the wings with your sauce, after throw them back onto the baking sheet and cook them for 5 more minutes.

Voila!! you got some delicous BBQ wings to eat for you and all your friends!
